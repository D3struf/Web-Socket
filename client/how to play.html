GATHER YOUR GROUP

The game works best with 3-10
players. Each player needs their
own computer.
START THE GAME

Invite your friends to join your
game by sharing the game code.
LOCATE YOUR TRIP

Each player must select only one
desired location to pin and then vote
for their favorites. The final location will
be determined by the highest number of
votes.
